TREE PLANTATION

A tree plantation, forest plantation, plantation forest, timber plantation
or tree farm is a forest planted for high volume production of wood,
usually by planting one type of tree as a monoculture forest.
The term tree farm also is used to refer to [[[Plant nursery|tree nurseries]] and Christmas tree farms.
